Here is a bit about what we do:

We provide a platform to help making parking in public places easy. Our app allows you to find parking spots in select locations all over your city. It allows you to see the availability of parking in places such as malls, museums, stadiums etc. We offer amazing discounts which you cannot turn down.

Price:

The price of your parking depends on the following factors:

  • type of vehicle(sedan or SUV)
  • location
  • time of the day
  • availability

Customers are charged by the hour and all payments are made online. We offer customes a variety of payment methods to ensure easy access to all. payments can be made via credit card, debit card, UPI, paytm and other online wallets. You cam also add money to your own parking wallet which makes the payment process easy. no cash payments


payments for the first hour must be made at the time of booking. The remaining amount(if any) will be deducted at the time of exit from the parking.
